Robo Expo 2016

The annual Robotics exhibition of ISM Dhanbad
What is expo?

Expo is the hall of fame of the best, baddest and most innovative technological project's created by student's of ISM Dhanbad. It is conducted by Robotics Club of ISM Dhanbad anually generally around the month of August and September. Every year, few best project's are shortlisted and exhibited in a two-day interctive exhibition. Not surprisingly, if attracts a huge amount of crowd consisting of curious students, proud professors and also civilians. The exhibition always gains media attention and lurking journalist's with big camera's and taking notes are a common sight.
Any student of ISM who is interested and having an innovative project can participate in Expo, just contact with Robotics Club member's for more information or leave a message here describing your project and contact information.
