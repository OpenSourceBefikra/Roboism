We at RoboISM do not differentiate on members on basis year. Anyone who is willing to learn or share his/her knowledge are more than welcome to come forward, express their views and participate.

However, membership of RoboISM is granted to a student only after a rigorous interview about his technical knowledge, past project and most importantly, enthusiasm about robotics. An interview is held for selection of first year member's generally around the month of september or october. Any year member can apply to be part of RoboISM all year round. Once in the club, students undergo various courses like programming, embedded, designing etc. according to their field of interest, and encouraged to make projects and participate in various Hackathons and Competitions.

We can fund our own projects or borrow materials from club's store.
